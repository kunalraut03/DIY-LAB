Our Services

Empowering innovation with cutting-edge tools and resources

At Vigyan Ashram's DIY Lab, we provide a range of cutting-edge tools, machines, and resources to fuel your creativity and enable innovation. Whether you're a student, maker, or entrepreneur, our services are designed to help you turn your ideas into reality.

3D Printers: Julia Advanced and Julia Extended

  • Transform ideas into tangible prototypes.
  • Rapidly create custom parts and designs.
  • Supports diverse materials for all needs.

Laser Cutting Machine

  • Precision cutting for wood, acrylic, fabrics.
  • Engrave intricate designs with ease.
  • Perfect for personal or professional projects.

Vinyl Cutting Machine

  • Create stickers, decals, and graphics.
  • Ideal for branding and promotional materials.
  • Delivers precise and professional results.

Laboratory Tools

  • Comprehensive tools for fabrication projects.
  • Includes drills, grinders, and hand tools.
  • Ensures precision with measuring instruments.

3D and 2D Software with Internet Access

  • Access platforms like Solidworks, Fusion 360, FreeCAD, Autodesk FUSION.
  • Design, simulate, and perfect projects seamlessly.
  • Stay connected for research and collaboration.
