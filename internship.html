Explore Internships

The DIY Lab at Vigyan Ashram is a hub for innovation, creativity, and hands-on learning. Whether you're an aspiring engineer, an innovator, or someone passionate about social development, our internship programs offer you the chance to explore, experiment, and create solutions that matter.

Our Internship Programs

1-Month Internship

Short-term

A Quick Dive into Innovation

In just around a month, immerse yourself in the vibrant world of the DIY Lab. Work on exciting projects and DIY activities, learn to use cutting-edge tools, and leave with new skills and a fresh perspective.

Who It's For:

Students and professionals seeking a short but impactful experience in hands-on innovation.

What You'll Do:
  • Learn the basics of 3D printing, IoT, electronics, and mechanical fabrication.
  • Work on small-scale projects solving real-world and day to day challenges.
  • Collaborate with mentors and peers to enhance your technical know-how.

6-Month Internship

Long-term

Master the Art of Making

For those ready to take a deep dive, this program offers the chance to become a skilled innovator and create meaningful impact. Over six months, you'll tackle larger projects and gain advanced expertise.

Who It's For:

Innovators and changemakers looking to gain in-depth knowledge and hands-on experience.

What You'll Do:
  • Develop projects in fields like renewable energy, smart agriculture, or sustainable technologies.
  • Learn advanced techniques in prototyping, IoT, and social technology development.
  • Collaborate with the community to implement your solutions.

Why Choose the DIY Lab Internship?

Practical Learning

Work with tools like 3D printers, laser cutters, and IoT devices.

Real-World Impact

Design solutions that improve social livelihoods and address critical challenges.

Expert Mentorship

Learn from experienced professionals dedicated to your growth.

Innovative Environment

Explore, experiment, and create in a supportive and collaborative space.
